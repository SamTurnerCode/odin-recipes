Canto Bight Martini

Description

The Canto Bight Martini is a luxurious and captivating cocktail inspired by the vibrant atmosphere of the renowned casino city in the Outer Rim on the planet of Cantonica. This saffron-infused twist on the classic Monte Carlo cocktail tantalizes your taste buds with its golden hue and exotic flavors. Sip on this elegant concoction and let the essence of Canto Bight transport you to a world of opulence and intrigue.

Ingredients

Cocktail

  • 60 milliliters premium gin
  • 15 milliliters dry vermouth
  • 15 milliliters saffron-infused simple syrup
  • Dash of orange bitters
  • Saffron threads (for garnish)
  • Lemon twist (for garnish)

Simple Syrup

  • A cup of water
  • A cup of raw sugar
  • pinch of saffron threads

Recipes

  1. Prepare the saffron-infused simple syrup in advance. In a small saucepan, combine water, sugar, and a pinch of saffron threads. Heat the mixture over medium heat until the sugar has dissolved completely, stirring occasionally. Allow the syrup to cool, and then strain out the saffron threads. Set the saffron-infused simple syrup aside for later use.
  2. Fill a mixing glass or cocktail shaker halfway with ice cubes.
  3. Pour the premium gin, dry vermouth, saffron-infused simple syrup, and a dash of orange bitters into the mixing glass or cocktail shaker.
  4. Stir the mixture gently for about 30 seconds to combine the ingredients thoroughly and chill the drink.
  5. Strain the cocktail into a chilled martini glass, using a cocktail strainer to remove any ice.
  6. Garnish the Canto Bight Martini with a few delicate saffron threads, allowing them to float atop the drink like golden stardust. Add a twist of lemon zest for a touch of citrus aroma and visual appeal.
  7. Serve the Canto Bight Martini immediately, allowing its mesmerizing colors and exquisite flavors to enchant your senses.
